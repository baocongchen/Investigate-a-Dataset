The correlation efficient of "Age" is -0.25 which means it's inversely propotional to "Survived". Let's use 1 tailed 2 sample t test to verify it. We want to test the hypothesis at the a = 0.05 level (95%)

H0: the average age of the survived population is less than that of the dead population.
HA: the average age of the survived population is equal or higher that of the dead population.
In other words:
H0: µ1 = µ2
HA: µ1 ≠ µ2
